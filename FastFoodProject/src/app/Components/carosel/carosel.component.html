Dragon Chicken

Dragon Chicken is a spicy and flavorful Indo-Chinese dish made with marinated and stir-fried chicken strips that are cooked with red chili sauce, bell peppers, onions, and various spices. It gets its name from its bold, fiery taste and the vibrant red appearance of the dish.
